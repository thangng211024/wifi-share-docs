Data Deletion Instructions

According to Facebook's Platform rules, we provide the following instructions for users to request the deletion of their data from WiFi Share: Find & Connect.

Method 1: Delete via the App (Recommended)

You can instantly delete your account and all associated data directly within the app:

  1. Open the WiFi Share app.
  2. Go to your Settings or Profile menu.
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account".
  4. Confirm your choice. Your data will be removed from our servers.

Method 2: Request via Email

If you no longer have the app installed, you can request manual data deletion by contacting our support team:

Email: thangng211024@gmail.com

Subject: Data Deletion Request

Please send this email from the address associated with your account, or include your exact profile name. We will process your request and delete your data within 7 business days.
